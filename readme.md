## Dataset Description

The coffee sales dataset includes the following columns:

- **Date** - The date of the transaction
- **DateTime** - The date and time of the transaction
- **Cash_type** - The payment method (Card or Cash)
- **Card** - Card payment indicator
- **Money** - The transaction amount
- **Coffee_name** - The type of coffee purchased

---

## Data Transformation

The following data transformations were applied:

- Changed data type of "date" column from general to date format
- Changed data type of "money" column from general to number format

## Data Visualisation & Key Insights

### 1. Total Sales Amount
**Total Sales: $36,805.78**

The dataset contains comprehensive sales records with a total sales amount of $36,805.78 across all transactions.

### 2. Total Transactions
**Total Transactions: 1,133**

A total of 1,133 transactions were recorded in the dataset, representing customer purchases over the analysis period.

### 3. Payment Method Distribution
**Card: 92%**  
**Cash: 8%**

The majority of customers (92%) prefer to pay by card, while only 8% of transactions are conducted in cash. This shows a strong preference for digital payment methods.

### 4. Best Selling Coffee
**Best Selling Coffee: Americano with Milk / Latte**

Based on both quantity and sales amount analysis, Americano with Milk and Latte products are the best-selling coffee types in the dataset.

### 5. Highest Revenue Generating Coffee
**Latte: $9,009.14**

Latte is the coffee type that generates the most revenue, with total sales of $9,009.14, making it the most valuable product in terms of revenue generation.

### 6. Monthly Sales Trend

| Month | Average Sales Amount |
|-------|---------------------|
| March | $34.22 |
| April | $34.29 |
| May | $33.95 |
| June | $34.18 |
| July | $29.18 |
| **Grand Total** | **$33.11** |

**Key Finding:** Sales were highest in the month of May. The trendline shows a slight upward trend in sales overall, with a notable dip in July.

### 7. Payment Method Percentage (Customer Split)
- **Card: 92%**
- **Cash: 8%**

Consistent with transaction volume, 92% of customers prefer card payments while 8% use cash.

### 8. Sales Forecast
A forecast based on past data has been generated to project future sales trends based on historical transaction patterns.

This analysis provides valuable insights into coffee shop sales performance:
